### Overview

Wiew your account info at [dime.io/account](https://dime.io/account).
You can update your password and manage your team.

### Adding team members

Click the + in the Team section, then enter the emails of anyone you
want to add to the account. We'll automatically send invitation emails
to them (they may need to check their spam folder).
